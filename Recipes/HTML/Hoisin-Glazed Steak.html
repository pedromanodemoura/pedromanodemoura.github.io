HOISIN-GLAZED STEAK

with Ginger Rice and Garlicky Green Beans


READY IN: 40 MIN

PREP IN: 10 MIN


INGREDIENTS

Ginger

(1/1 Thumb)

Garlic

(2/4 Cloves)

Yellow Onion

(1/2)

Sirloin Steak

(10/20 oz)

Jasmine Rice

(¾/1½ tsp)


Green Beans

(6/12 oz)

Beef Stock Concentrate

(1/2)

Hoisin Sauce

(1/2)

Butter

(1/2 tbsp)

Vegetable Oil

(6/10 tsp)


INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Wash and dry all produce. Halve, peel, and finely chop onion until you have ¼ cup. Peel ginger, then mince until you have 1 TBSP. Peel and mince 2 cloves garlic. Pat steak dry with a paper towel and season all over with salt and pepper.

  2. Heat a drizzle of oil in a small pot over medium heat. Add ginger and half the garlic. Cook until beginning to brown, 1-2 minutes. Add rice to pot and toss to coat.

  3. Pour 1¼ cups water and a large 1 pinch of salt into pot. Cover, lower heat, and reduce to a gentle simmer. Cook until tender, 15-20 minutes. Meanwhile, heat a drizzle of oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add green beans and cook, tossing occasionally, until tender, about 6 minutes. TIP: Add a splash of water to pan if green beans won't soften.

  4. While veggies cook, place steak in a medium bowl and pat dry with paper towels. Add remaining seasoning, remaining lime zest, remaining garlic, and a large drizzle of oil. Season generously with salt and pepper; toss to coat.

  5. Toss remaining garlic into pan with green beans and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds. Season with salt and pepper. Remove everything from pan and set aside, covered, to keep warm. Heat 1 TBSP oil in same pan over medium-high heat. Add steak and cook to desired doneness, 4-7 minutes per side. Remove from pan and set aside. Wipe out any blackened bits in pan.

  6. Add Onion and a drizzle of oil to same pan over medium heat. Cook until softened, about 3 minutes. Stir in ⅓ cup water and stock con- centrate. Simmer, scraping up any browned bits, until thickened slightly, 1-2 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in hoisin sauce and 1 TBSP butter. Season with pepper.

  7. Fluff rice with a fork and season with salt. Thinly slice steak. Divide rite, steak and green beans between plates. Drizzle sauce over steak and serve.
