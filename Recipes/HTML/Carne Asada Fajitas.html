CARNE ASADA FAJITAS

with Pickled Onions and Lime Crema


READY IN: 45 MIN

PREP IN: 10 MIN


INGREDIENTS

Lime

(1/2)

Green Bell Pepper

(1/2)

Red Onion

(1/2)

Garlic

(2/4 Cloves)

Sour Cream

(1/2)


Diced Steak

(1/2)

Southwest Seasoning

(1/2)

Flour Tortillas

(1/2)

Sugar

(¼/½ tsp)

Vegetable Oil

(4/8 tsp)


CHEF's TIP

In Step 6, we instruct you to warm your tortillas in the microwave. This yields steamy-soft wraps. To add a little more depth to your dish, try charring the tortillas over the flames of your gas stove. Before Step 5, using tongs and working one at a time, hold tortillas directly over the flames until they blister, for a few minutes per side.


INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Wash and dry all produce. Remove bell pepper stem and seeds, then thinly slice. Halve, peel, and very thinly slice onion. Zest 1 tsp zest from lime; cut into quarters. Peel and finely chop garlic.

  2. In a small bowl, combine ¼ of the onion, half the lime juice, ¼ tsp sugar, and a pinch of salt. Set aside to quick-pickle. In another small bowl, combine sour cream, half the lime zest, a squeeze of lime, and a pinch of garlic (to taste). Season with salt and pepper.

  3. Heat a drizzle of oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add bell pepper and season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring, until beginning to soften, about 4 minutes. Add remaining onion and season with more salt and pepper. Cook, stirring, until tender and lightly browned, 4-5 minutes more. Add 1 tsp seasoning (we sent more) and cook until fragrant, 30 seconds. Transfer to a plate and set aside.

  4. While veggies cook, place steak in a medium bowl and pat dry with paper towels. Add remaining seasoning, remaining lime zest, remaining garlic, and a large drizzle of oil. Season generously with salt and pepper; toss to coat.

  5. After veggies are done cooking, heat a drizzle of oil in same pan over high heat. Add steak and cook, stirring once or twice, until browned and cooked through, about 2 minutes. (Don't move the steak around too much! You want to develop a nice sear on all sides.)

  6. While steak cooks, wrap tortillas in a slightly damp paper towel and microwave until warm and pliable, about 30 seconds. Fill each tortilla with some steak, veggies, pickled onions, and a drizzle of lime crema. Serve with remaining lime wedge for squeezing over.
