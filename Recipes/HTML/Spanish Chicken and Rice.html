SPANISH CHICKEN AND RICE

with Poblano Pepper and Paprika Crema


READY IN: 30 MIN

PREP IN: 10 MIN


INGREDIENTS

Chicken Breasts

(12/24 oz)

Fry Seasoning

(1/2 tbsp)

Smoked Paprika

(1/2 tsp)

Poblano Pepper

(1/2)

Yellow Onion

(1/2)


Garlic

(3/6 Cloves)

Jasmine Rice

(¾/1½ Cups)

Parsley

(1/1)

Sour Cream

(2/4)

Butter

(1/2 tbsp)

Vegetable Oil

(1/2 tbsp)


INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Wash and dry all produce. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Pat chicken dry with a paper towel and season all over with salt, pepper, half the fry season- ing, and half the smoked paprika (we'll use more of the spices later).

  2. Heat 1 TBSP oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until browned on surface, about 3 minutes per side.

  3. Transfer chicken to a baking sheet. Bake in oven until no longer pink in center, 6-10 minutes. Meanwhile, core and seed poblano, then cut into ½-inch squares. Halve, peel, and dice onion. Peel and mince 2 cloves garlic.

  4. Place 1 TBSP butter, onion, and garlic in pan used for chicken over medi- um-high heat. Cook, tossing, until beginning to soften, about 3 minutes. Add poblano and cook, tossing, until beginning to soften, 3 minutes more. Season with salt and pep- per. Add rice and remaining fry seasoning, tossing to coat. Stir in 1¼ cups water and a large pinch of salt. Bring to a boil. Cover, lower heat, and reduce to a gentle simmer. Cook until rice is tender, 15-20 minutes.

  5. Remove chicken from oven once done and let rest 5 minutes before thinly slicing. Peel another clove of garlic and mince until you have ¼ tsp. Finely chop parsley. In a small bowl, combine 2 packets sour cream, ¼ tsp smoked paprika (you'll have some left over), 1 TBSP water, and a pinch of garlic (add more to taste). Season with salt and pepper.

  6. Fluff rice with a fork. Season with salt and pepper, if needed. Arrange chicken on top and drizzle with sour cream mixture. Sprinkle over parsley and serve from pan.
